## 2025

### January

- [**o3-mini released**](https://openai.com/index/openai-o3-mini/)
- [**DeepSeek R1 released**](https://api-docs.deepseek.com/news/news250120)

### February

- Andrej Karpathy [coins "vibe coding"](https://x.com/karpathy/status/1886192184808149383)
- [**Claude 3.7 Sonnet released**](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-3-7-sonnet)
- [**Claude Code preview**](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-3-7-sonnet)

### April

- [**o3 and o4-mini released**](https://openai.com/index/introducing-o3-and-o4-mini/)
- [**GPT-4.1 series released**](https://openai.com/index/gpt-4-1/)
- [Slopsquatting](https://mastodon.social/@andrewnez/114302875075999244) term coined (by Seth Larson, amplified by Andrew Nesbitt)
- [Sonatype Q1 2025 report](https://www.sonatype.com/blog/open-source-malware-index-q1-2025): 17,954 malicious OSS packages identified; 56% related to data exfiltration (up from 26% in Q4 2024)

### May

- [**Claude Sonnet 4 and Opus 4 released**](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-4)
- [**Claude Code GA**](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-4)

- Open Collective reports being ["flooded with AI garbage"](https://techcrunch.com/2025/07/24/ai-slop-and-fake-reports-are-exhausting-some-security-bug-bounties/) bug reports

### June

- Trend Micro publishes [slopsquatting research](https://www.trendmicro.com/vinfo/us/security/news/cybercrime-and-digital-threats/slopsquatting-when-ai-agents-hallucinate-malicious-packages)
- [Malicious PyPI packages targeting Solana developers](https://www.reversinglabs.com/blog/same-name-different-hack-pypi-package-targets-solana-developers) discovered

### July

- curl bug report volume [spikes to 8x normal rate](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/2025/07/14/death-by-a-thousand-slops/) in one week
- Stenberg publicly [mulls killing the bug bounty program](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/2025/07/14/death-by-a-thousand-slops/)
- [HackerOne launches Hai Triage](https://www.hackerone.com/press-release/hackerone-unveils-hai-triage-upgraded-ai-powered-vulnerability-response)
- PyPI publishes [incident report on phishing attack](https://blog.pypi.org/posts/2025-07-31-incident-report-phishing-attack/)
- [IndonesianFoods campaign](https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/security/new-indonesianfoods-spammer-floods-npm-with-150-000-packages/) reaches 150,000+ npm packages
- METR study: [experienced open-source developers are 19% slower with AI tools](https://metr.org/blog/2025-07-10-early-2025-ai-experienced-os-dev-study/) — despite believing they were 20% faster

### August

- [**GPT-5 released**](https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-5/)
- [**Claude Opus 4.1 released**](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-opus-4-1)

### September

- [**Claude Sonnet 4.5 released**](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-sonnet-4-5)

- PyPI publishes [incident report on token exfiltration via GitHub Actions](https://blog.pypi.org/posts/2025-09-16-github-actions-token-exfiltration/)
- Cybersecurity Dive: ["Tech giants pledged millions to secure open-source code. Then AI came along."](https://www.cybersecuritydive.com/news/open-source-software-security-progress-roadblocks/758506/)

### October

- [**Claude Haiku 4.5 released**](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-haiku-4-5)

## 2026

### January

- [Clawdbot renamed to Moltbot](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46783863) after Anthropic trademark complaint; then renamed to [OpenClaw](https://docs.openclaw.ai/) three days later. The abandoned "Moltbot" name was [immediately impersonated](https://www.malwarebytes.com/blog/threat-intel/2026/01/clawdbots-rename-to-moltbot-sparks-impersonation-campaign) by attackers who grabbed old handles and pushed [400+ malicious agent skills](https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/security/malicious-moltbot-skills-used-to-push-password-stealing-malware/) through ClawHub
- [Sonatype 2026 State of the Software Supply Chain report](https://www.sonatype.com/state-of-the-software-supply-chain/introduction) published
- Cloudflare publishes ["Building a serverless, post-quantum Matrix homeserver"](https://blog.cloudflare.com/serverless-matrix-homeserver-workers/); [Matrix Foundation responds](https://matrix.org/blog/2026/01/28/matrix-on-cloudflare-workers/)
- Armin Ronacher (Flask creator) publishes ["Agent Psychosis"](https://lucumr.pocoo.org/2026/1/18/agent-psychosis/)
- [Linus Torvalds tells kernel devs to stop making an issue of AI slop in docs](https://www.phoronix.com/news/Torvalds-Linux-Kernel-AI-Slop)
- curl [shuts down bug bounty program](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/2026/01/26/the-end-of-the-curl-bug-bounty/) (effective Jan 31)
- [LLVM institutes "human-in-the-loop" policy](https://github.com/llvm/llvm-project/commit/18695b27d565) for AI-generated contributions
- Ghostty (Hashimoto) [escalates to zero-tolerance AI policy](https://github.com/ghostty-org/ghostty/pull/10412)
- tldraw (Steve Ruiz) [auto-closes ALL external pull requests](https://tldraw.dev/blog/stay-away-from-my-trash) — "Stay away from my trash"
- [128 phantom npm packages](https://www.aikido.dev/blog/agent-skills-spreading-hallucinated-npx-commands) (slopsquatting) racked up 121,539 downloads Jul 2025–Jan 2026
- ["How AI Impacts Skill Formation"](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.20245) (Shen & Tamkin) — developers using AI learned less and debugged worse, without significant efficiency gains

### February

- Daniel Stenberg delivers FOSDEM 2026 closing keynote: ["Open Source Security in spite of AI"](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/2026/02/03/open-source-security-in-spite-of-ai/)
- [International AI Safety Report 2026](https://internationalaisafetyreport.org/publication/international-ai-safety-report-2026) published (Yoshua Bengio et al.) — documents AI agents completing multi-hour software engineering tasks autonomously
- [**Claude Opus 4.6 released**](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-opus-4-6) — Anthropic reports it [found 500+ high-severity zero-day vulnerabilities](https://www.axios.com/2026/02/05/anthropic-claude-opus-46-software-hunting) in OSS libraries (Ghostscript, OpenSC, CGIF) during [internal red-team testing](https://red.anthropic.com/2026/zero-days/)
- [GitHub weighs PR "kill switch"](https://www.theregister.com/2026/02/03/github_kill_switch_pull_requests_ai/)
- RedMonk publishes ["AI Slopageddon and the OSS Maintainers"](https://redmonk.com/kholterhoff/2026/02/03/ai-slopageddon-and-the-oss-maintainers/)
- InfoWorld: ["Is AI killing open source?"](https://www.infoworld.com/article/4129056/is-ai-killing-open-source.html)
- continue.dev: ["We're Losing Open Contribution"](https://blog.continue.dev/were-losing-open-contribution/)
- Mitchell Hashimoto releases [Vouch](https://github.com/mitchellh/vouch), a community trust management system based on explicit vouches to participate
- [1Password launches SCAM benchmark](https://1password.com/blog/ai-agent-security-benchmark) — tests whether AI agents fall for phishing and social engineering during realistic workflows; every model tested committed critical failures
- [An AI Agent Published a Hit Piece on Me](https://theshamblog.com/an-ai-agent-published-a-hit-piece-on-me/)
- [Gentoo launches official Codeberg mirror](https://www.gentoo.org/news/2026/02/16/codeberg.html) as an alternative to GitHub for contributions
